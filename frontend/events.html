May 19
Bookworm: A Literary Journey Through Art

by Creative Canvas Co.

19 May 10:00 AM - 22 May 5:00 PM


Step into a world where words come alive through brushstrokes and colors in "Bookworm: A
Literary Journey Through Art." This captivating exhibition celebrates the timeless bond
between literature and visual expression, inviting visitors on an enchanting exploration of
beloved stories, characters, and the magic of books.

Wander through the gallery and immerse yourself in scenes inspired by iconic literary
works. Lose yourself in the whimsical landscapes of fantasy realms, where dragons soar
and mythical creatures roam. Feel the romance and drama unfold in vivid portrayals of love
stories and historical epics. Encounter the haunting mysteries of gothic tales, where
shadows dance and secrets lurk in every corner.
